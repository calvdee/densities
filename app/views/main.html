How do I use this? Select a density function from the dropdown and click inside one of the parameter boxes then experiment with pressing or holding the up/down arrows on your keyboard. This will cause the density function to be recomputed with the new parameter setting(s), creating a transition that brings the density function to life!

What are the icons for? They are links to Wikipedia which describe the selected density function and their parameters.

How can I close this notification? By clicking 'x' in the upper right corner
